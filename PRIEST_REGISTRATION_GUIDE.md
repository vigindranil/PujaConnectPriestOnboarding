# Priest Registration Step-by-Step Process

## Overview
The PujaConnect Agent Dashboard now includes a comprehensive **5-step priest registration process** that guides agents through registering new priests in the system.

## Registration Steps

### Step 1: Basic Information
Collects essential personal information about the priest.

**Fields:**
- **Full Name** (Required) - Priest/Pandit's full name
- **Phone Number** (Required) - 10-digit mobile number
- **Email Address** (Required) - Email for communication
- **Age** (Required) - Age of the priest (18-100)
- **Gender** (Required) - Male, Female, or Other

**Validation:** All fields must be filled to proceed to the next step.

---

### Step 2: Professional Details
Captures professional experience and expertise.

**Fields:**
- **Years of Experience** (Required) - Total years of priestly experience
- **Education/Qualification** (Required) - E.g., "Vedic Scholar", "B.A. in Sanskrit"
- **Location/City** (Required) - Where the priest operates
- **Specializations** (Required) - Multiple specializations can be added
  - Click the input field, type a specialization, and click "Add"
  - Examples: Vedic Rituals, Marriage Ceremonies, Havan, Puja Ceremonies, etc.
  - Each specialization appears as a badge that can be removed by clicking the × button

**Validation:** All fields required, at least one specialization needed.

---

### Step 3: Qualifications & Languages
Documents formal qualifications and language abilities.

**Fields:**
- **Highest Qualification** (Required) - E.g., "Master's in Vedic Studies"
- **Certifications** (Required) - Professional certifications
  - Add certifications one by one
  - Examples: Vedic Ritual Certificate, Marriage Ceremony Specialist, Astrology Certificate
  - Click "Add" button after entering each certification
  - Remove certifications using the × button on badges
- **Languages Spoken** (Required) - Languages the priest can communicate in
  - Select from dropdown: Hindi, English, Sanskrit, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Gujarati, Marathi, Punjabi
  - Click "Add" to add selected language
  - Multiple languages can be selected

**Validation:** Highest qualification required, at least one language must be added.

---

### Step 4: Bank Account Details
Collects payment information for commission payouts.

**Fields:**
- **Account Holder Name** (Required) - Name as per bank account
- **Account Number** (Required) - Valid bank account number
- **IFSC Code** (Required) - Bank's IFSC code (e.g., SBIN0001234)
  - Automatically converts to uppercase
- **Account Type** (Optional) - Savings (default) or Current

**Important Note:**
- Bank details are used for monthly commission payouts
- Ensure all information is accurate
- Commission is earned per approved puja booking

**Validation:** Account holder name, account number, and IFSC code are mandatory.

---

### Step 5: Review & Submit
Final review of all entered information before submission.

**Shows:**
- **Basic Information** - Name, Phone, Email, Age, Gender
- **Professional Details** - Experience, Education, Location, Specializations
- **Bank Details** - Account holder name (masked account number), IFSC code
- **Qualifications** - Languages and certifications

**Confirmation:**
- Review all information carefully
- Check the confirmation checkbox:
  > "I certify that the information provided is accurate and complete. I agree to the terms and conditions of PujaConnect."
- Note: Information will be verified by admin team before approval

**Submit:** Click "Submit Registration" to complete the process.

---

## Registration Status & Workflow

### Status States
After registration, priests go through the following states:

1. **Pending** (⏳) - Awaiting admin approval
   - Registration submitted by agent
   - Admin team verifying documents and information
   
2. **Approved** (✅) - Active in the system
   - Priest can receive bookings
   - Commission earned per approved puja
   
3. **Rejected** (❌) - Declined by admin
   - Reasons typically: Missing certifications, incomplete information
   - Can re-submit registration if needed

### Commission System
- **Commission Rate:** ₹5,000 per approved puja booking
- **Payment Method:** Direct transfer to registered bank account
- **Frequency:** Monthly payouts
- **Visibility:** Commission earned shown on dashboard

## Dashboard Features

### After Registration
1. **View** - See priest details in read-only mode
2. **Edit** - Modify priest information (except basic details like name/phone)
3. **Delete** - Remove priest registration (with confirmation)
4. **Search** - Find priests by name, phone, or email
5. **Filter** - View by status (All, Approved, Pending, Rejected)

### Statistics Dashboard
- **Total Registered** - All priests registered by agent
- **Approved** - Priests earning commission
- **Pending** - Awaiting admin approval
- **Rejected** - Denied registrations
- **Commission Earned** - Total ₹ from all approved priests

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Proceed to Next Step?
- Ensure all required fields (marked with *) are filled
- Check for proper formatting (phone: 10 digits, IFSC: uppercase)
- Add at least one item for specializations/certifications/languages

### Registration Rejected?
- Check admin feedback (if provided)
- Ensure certifications are valid and current
- Verify bank account details are correct
- Re-submit with complete information

### How to Edit Registered Priest?
- Click the **Edit** (pencil) icon on the priests table
- Modify information (can't edit name/phone as per system rules)
- Click "Save Changes" to update
